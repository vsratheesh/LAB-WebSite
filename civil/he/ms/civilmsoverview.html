Masters in Structural Engineering

Civil Engineering Engineering is an arena which includes designing, constructing, and upholding the natural and physical atmosphere.Constructions made by Civil Engineers Engineers include barriers, channels, tunnels, and bridges or any closely man-made construction. Structural Engineering,on the other hand, is the study and design of  structures which fight or support loads; safe guarding them so that they will not breakdown and can endure forces touching them.

  • University of Southern California, USA

TOEFL:A minimum score of 565 generally containing a minimum score of 4.5 in writing and TOEFL IBT: A minimum score of 85 generally including a minimum score of 17 in Reading, Listening and Speaking and 19 in Writing.

Career Prospects and placements

Structural Engineers design the master plan framework of buildings, pipelines and bridges so that they can sustenance their own burden and fight forces, such as weather and traffic. A civil engineer may design roadways, tunnels, dams, building, bridges but the Structural engineering take care of specific architectural components like beams columns, foundations, floors and other structural components. Adding to public safety worries, they must also take into concern aesthetics and cost efficiency. They are often specialized in the types of structures they design and may work on projects ranging from residential complexes to mega structures.

According to information from Bureau of Labour Statistics, a median annual salary of civil engineers ranging from $70,000*to $90,000*, and the demand for civil and structural engineers is very higher than any other engineers.
