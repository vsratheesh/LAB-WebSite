    Why people prefer working?

  • The main and simple reason why we study is to acquire the knowledge, but in our country we study to secure our job.
  • Most of the students prefer working for a few years and then go for higher studies, reason may be they are not from a good financial background or they want to work few years and then go for higher studies.
  • Some people will have a passion of doing job immediately after B.Tech, so they will try to find all the options from their B.Tech in order to succeed in their first job search.
  • Few people will think like having some work experience will help them in higher studies.
What is your choice?

It is easy to get a job in on campus drive than in walking and off-campus drives. These days most of the students are looking for IT jobs irrespective of their branch. But, in the core sector there are many options available for Civil engineering graduates such as 2D modelling (AutoCAD), 3D modelling (CATIA, UNI GRAPHICS ,PRO E / CREO) etc..

By attending training for any of these courses you can easily get a core job as there are many jobs available in the core sector.
