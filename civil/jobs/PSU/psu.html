Overview

Public Sector Undertaking Jobs (PSU’s)

The only jobs that provide a constant source of income are government jobs. PSU’s allow you to get into core sector. If you are into PSUs you get a chance to improve your skills that were learnt in engineering. The possible reasons why people are interested in getting into PSUs are

  • Attractive basic salary which is around 8Lakhs/annum
  • Minimal work pressure
  • Job security

Though many B.Tech graduates want to get into PSU’s, they really do not have any idea about this sector. Further in this article you will read types of PSU’s, the recruitment procedures, eligibility, career growth etc,.

PSUs are divided into 3 categories based on their yearly turn-over of the organization. They are

  • Maharatna (7)
  • Miniratna (17)
  • Navaratana (56)
Ten Things

Eligibility criteria

Academic Qualification: B.Tech in Civil Engineering

Age Limit: Varies from company to company but generally lies between 21 and 30 years.

Work Experience: Not required for many posts but adds weight during interview and also helps during posting.

Physical Standards: Not general physical standards are mentioned. Standards May vary from role to role, may be high for floor jobs.

How to Enter

Entrance Exam: General English, General Awareness, Current Affairs and Civil Engineering.

Apply Directly: Students can apply directly to companies which specify the mode of application and entrance exam in the notification.

Through A Reference: Depends on company. Some companies accept applications through reference.

How to Prepare

Written Exam:

  • General Studies/GK – Manorama Year Book
  • General English – R S Sharma.
  • Civil Engineering – Engineering books.

Personal Interview: Only some PSU/PSEs have personal interviews. These interviews are generally purely technical in nature. You should also read about company profile.

Skill Set

These jobs are primarily technical in nature. However, those who are selected as management trainees will become managers in future. Those selected as MT will be trained by the company as they progress in their career.

Job Description and Designation

Graduate Engineer, Management Trainee, Engineer Trainee and Executive Trainee are the entry-level designation. Fresh recruits are expected to perform technical tasks such as production, design, etc. of machines.

Salary and Perks

Salary and perks vary from company to company. Minimum salary is Rs. 25,000 and may go up to Rs. 70,000/- in Maharatna and Navaratna companies. Some top PSUs such as Indian Oil Corporation (IOC) have unparalleled perks.

Career Progression

Engineer, Senior Engineer, Assistant Managers, Managers etc

The Don’ts

  • Don’tlose patience. In PSUs, career growth may not be as fast as in private companies.
  • Don’t despair with posting in remote areas. Though in initial years, you’ll be posted in remote areas, as you grow in career, you’ll be posted in metro areas.

Career Switch

  • IES offers opportunity to work at different levels in government. If you wish to move to other career, you will have the option of entering the private sector, become a professor or go for higher studies such as Ph.D abroad.
Job Roles
  • Operations
  • Project planning or Coordination and Management
  • Billing or commercials and contracts

Operations

Operation or Execution

In this profile one will be deployed as a site engineer and role will be high level or low level based on the work.

Generally monitored by a construction manager; mostly a fresher job will be all about labour supply, requirement and deployment based on tangibility. Also one will be deployed on daily operations related tasks

Some roles include preparation of daily site activity report, bar bending, schedule preparation, productivity monitoring, subcontractor man head count and measurement and guiding the foremen/supervisors as per the plan and also to provide technical insight when needed.

Project planning or Coordination and Management

Planning is all about daily plans, weekly plans, monthly plans, quarterly plans etc. After a few months of experience, depending upon the grasping speed, one is asked to create such plans and projection. Other plans include mobilization and demobilizations plans, formwork planning, material resource planning, labour resource planning, staff scheduling, etc.

  • Coming to coordination part, it includes coordination with the owner, consultants and project Management Company for drawing, detailing site instructions etc.
  • Planning Manager is also considered as the second man to the project manager and is responsible for coordinating with all other departments.

Billing or commercials and contracts

  • Billing of the activities carried out as per the billing cycle and getting it certified and paid by the owner.
  • Activity costing, overall costing, budgeting, estimation, Cost till date, Estimate to complete and P&L statements.
  • Non-scheduled and extra item rate analysis, claims, etc. Cost control and contract administration.
Companies List

First 6 months a fresher will be exposed to the mentioned areas and specializations. This training schedule will be same for the entire recruited fresher’s in the organization. Once after completing this modular training, one will have that opportunity to select a specific department.

Some of the PSUs that hire core civil engineers:

  • NTPC
  • SAIL
  • HPCL
  • IOCL
  • RITES
  • NHAI etc

Public sector undertakings or public sector enterprise are State-Owned enterprises in India. PSUs recruit Civil Engineers for various posts at entry level like

  • Graduate Engineer
  • Management Trainee
  • Executive Trainee
  • Engineer Trainee, etc.

Recruitments for these posts will be on GATE score for most of the companies and other companies conduct their own entrance exam based on their requirements for selecting candidates.

How to prepare

Most of the PSU companies recruit students on the basis of GATE score. So you need to prepare well for GATE exam. As the competition is very tough you need to put the best efforts to get the job in PSUs. Apart from core subjects you need to learn general awareness, English and aptitude, in civil engineering most important topics are strength of material, manufacturing, applied thermodynamics.

If your aim is to get in to PSUs make sure that you get a rank below 200 in GATE. To get good rank in GATE you have to answer maximum questions and score more. While preparing for GATE it is advisable not to skip any subjects to get good score. Try to solve previous year GATE papers it will help you a lot, you will get clear idea about important topics and difficulty level by solving previous papers. Always revise the studied topics.

PSUs majorly hire through GATE, though they have immediate requirement they will hire through previous GATE score and suppose to attend Personal or Group discussion, but the short listing criteria will be GATE rank.

Selection Process

National Thermal Power Corporation Limited (NTPC)

NTPC recruits Civil engineers for the post of Executive engineer based on the GATE score through NTPC Engineers Executive Trainee (EET) Exam. The students will be called for the Group discussion and interview in the ratio of 1:3. Student with 65% marks (General/OBC) 55% for SC/ST/PWD are eligible to apply. Notification for this post will be in the month of September or October Selected candidates will be placed in the pay scale of Rs. 24900-50500 at the basic pay of Rs. 24900/- during one-year training. These candidates will be absorbed after successful completion of training as Assistant Manager in the pay scale of Rs. 24900-3%-50500 at a basic pay of Rs. 25650/-

Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Ltd. (ONGC)

ONGC is a Maharatna PSU company. ONGC hires Civil engineers for the post of Graduate Trainee (GT) based on GATE score. Students will be called for the interview in the ratio of 1:3. Notification for this post will be in the month of November and December. A student with 60 % aggregate is eligible for this post. The pay scale is in the grade of Rs. 24,900 – 50,500/- with an increment of 3% per year.

Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited

HPCL hires Civil engineers for the Graduate Engineer (GE) position. Students will be called based on GATE score and then company conducts group discussion and Interview. Eligibility for the candidate is minimum 60% aggregate for general/OBC and 50% for others.CTC will be around 8.5 lakhs per annum.

National Aluminum Company Limited (NALCO)

NALCO is a Navaratna company which hires Civil engineers for the Graduate Engineers (GE) position. Students will be called based on GATE score and then company conducts group discussion and Interview. Notification for this post will be in the month of August and September. Eligibility for the candidate is minimum 65% aggregate for general/OBC and 55% for others.CTC at minimum Pay scale is Rs. 27027/- per month.

Indian Oil Corporation Ltd.

IOCL hires civil students for the post of Graduate engineers on the basis of GATE score further selection process will be group discussion and personnel interview. Notification will be released in August or September. Candidates with 65% aggregate are eligible. CTC is around 9 lakhs per annum.

Steel Authority of India Limited

SAIL hires civil graduates for post of Management Trainee. Candidates with 55% aggregate and age below 30 years are eligible to apply. Selection will be based on written test and personnel interview. The candidates who scored 50 percentile marks in written exam are eligible for interview and GD. Pay scale for this post is around 20,600 to 46,500/- per month.

Delhi Metro Rail Corporation Ltd

DMRC hires civil graduates for post of Jr. Engineer. Candidates with 60% aggregate and age below 30 years are eligible to apply. Selection will be based on GATE, GD and personnel interview. Pay scale for this post is around 13,500 to 25,520/- per month.

Vizag Steel Plant

Vizag Steel plant hires civil graduates for post of Management Trainee (Technical). Candidates with 60% aggregate and age below 40 years are eligible to apply. Selection will be based on written test, GD and personnel interview. Pay scale for this post is around 206000 to 46500/- per month.

Heavy Engineering Corporation Limited (HECL)

HECL hires civil graduates for post of Technical Graduate Engineers. Candidates with 60% aggregate are eligible to apply. Selection will be based on GATE and personnel interview. Pay scale for this post is around 16000 to 40000/- per month. .

Delhi Development Authority (DDA)

DDA hires civil graduates for post of Technical Graduate Engineers. Candidates with 60% aggregate are eligible to apply. Selection will be based on GATE and personnel interview. Pay scale for this post is around 15,600 to 39,100/- per month.

Rail India Technical and Economic Service (RITES)

RITES, a PSU of Government of India which will be recruit civil graduates as Site engineers, with a pay of Rs. 20,600 – 46,500/ per month. Starting basic pay of Rs. 20,600/- and additional increments can be given to the deserving candidates. Candidates should have total post-qualification experience of minimum 6 years, out of which at least 2 years should be in relevant field

Electronics Corporation of India Limited (ECIL)

ECIL hires civil graduates for post of Graduate Engineers. Candidates with 65% aggregate are eligible to apply. Selection will be based on Written and personnel interview. Pay scale for this post is around 4 lakhs per annum.

Pros&Cons

Pros

  • There is no extra working hours and stable source of income.

  • Promotions based on your work.

  • Pension facility is available when you retire.

  • Free medical facility for you and your family members with minimum accommodation charges.

  • Being a Govt employee you will have reorganization/status in society.

Cons

  • Salary growth will be slow when compared with IT sector.

  • If you have any ambition in your life to do something new you cannot achieve that as you are in to Govt job.

  • You have to do respective work every day, jobs will not be challenging to you.

  • You might have to deal with corruption in some form or the other.
