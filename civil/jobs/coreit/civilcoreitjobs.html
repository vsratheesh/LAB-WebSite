Overview

Core - IT Jobs

A Civil engineer is not restricted to manufacturing /construction or allied sectors. As said earlier, there are many fields where a civil engineer’s knowledge is applicable. In design, production, manufacturing, supply-chain management and maintenance of machines etc. To understand and implement such projects, one is expected to have knowledge in both civil engineering and IT.

Many people after pursuing their B.Tech are going either for higher studies or for IT jobs but core jobs are neglected because lack of information. When we asked people about core jobs their answers were like

  • Very few options available
  • We are trying but no response from the companies even after cracking the interview.
  • No large openings for Civil in core companies
  • They will not offer high salaries like IT
  • Huge competition of a single post

Here we give you the different core jobs that are available, how to get through them, eligibility, career growth, pay pack, etc,.

Ten Things

Eligibility criteria

Academic Qualification:B.Tech in Civil Engineering

Age Limit: No specific limit mentioned but generally between 21 and 25 years

Work Experience: For roles such as ERP consultant (Eg: Oracle Apps), prior work experience of at least 2 years is required in Civil domain (SCM, manufacturing, production, procurement etc).

How to enter?

Engineering materials, Structural analysis etc.

Apply Directly: Generally, one needs to pass in the recruitment test even when one applies directly to the company.

Through A Reference: In small companies, you can get a job through reference. In some MNCs, special employee referral drives are conducted sometimes while some big companies only accept through recruitment test.

How to prepare?

Written Exam: Aptitude and Reasoning – R S Agarwal

Any tool knowledge – AutoCAD, Staad Pro, TopoCAD, SurvCAD, AutoTurn etc.

(Eg: Java Scripting) as they are used for designing

Personal Interview: Revise your core subjects thoroughly and gain knowledge on the company (such as company profile, clients, product line, etc.)

Skill Set

You need to be good with at least a few software tools (such as AutoCAD, Solid Works, CATIA, etc.) in addition to knowing your core subjects. You are also expected to know the basics of computers thoroughly.

Salary and Perks

Salary ranges from 1.5 lakh to 5 lakh depending on the company. One of the most important perks of working in Civil is that you get to stay in cities. Some companies even provide transport, house, with good pay etc but only when the site is in remote locations.

Designing

Civil engineers use drafting (also known as technical drawing) in designing of products and creating instruction for the parts of machines. A technical drawing essentially includes all the dimensions to manufacture a part. It can either be hand-drawn or computer-modelled.

A technical drawing can be a computer model or hand-drawn schematic showing all the dimensions necessary to manufacture a part, as well as assembly notes, a list of required materials, and other pertinent information.

2D Modeling

AutoCAD software:

It helps in 2D drafting and 2D drawing helps one to create and edit technical drawings, as well as annotate designs. These tools are used by drafters in activities such as development of floor plans, building permit drawings, building inspection plans, and landscaping layouts.

  • Draft designs more quickly, without using stencils and technical drawing instruments
  • Develop site plans, refine concepts, sketch technical drawings, and share ideas
  • Customize templates and drawings more precisely

3D Modeling:

It is the 3D computer graphics,3D modeling (or modeling) is the process of developing a mathematical representation of any three-dimensional  surface of an object (either inanimate or living) via specialized software The product is called a 3D model. It can be displayed as a two-dimensional image through a process called 3D rendering  or used in a computer simulation  of physical phenomena. The model can also be physically created using 3D printing  devices.

Models may be created automatically or manually. The manual modeling process of preparing geometric data for 3D computer graphics is similar to plastic arts  such as sculpting.
