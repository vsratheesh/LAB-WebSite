What is Mech?

Mechanical Engineering is the largest of all engineering branches,including zones such as energy, fluid mechanics, dynamics, combustion, vibration, design, manufacturing process, systems modeling and replication, mechatronics, robotics, mechanics of material, rapid protottyping and combinations.

Mechanical engineering involves the application of generation, distribution, and use of energy; the processing of materials; the control and automation of manufacturing systems; the design and development of machines; and the solutions to environmental problems.
