What is ECE?

All the applications which craft our everyday life easier and pleasurable such as TV, Mobiles, Computers, Home Appliances, Radio and many more are designed and urbanized by
Electronics and Communication Engineers.

ECE’s deals with the circuits, communication equipments like transmitter, integrated circuits (IC) and receiver and electronic devices. It also deals with fundamental electronics, analog and digital broadcast & data response, voice and video like AM, FM and DTH, antennae and wave development, satellite communications, microprocessors, and microwave engineering,. It targets to intensify the awareness and skills of the students on the essential concepts and theories that will provide them in their specialized work connecting analysis, systems implementation, function, manufacture, and upholding a variety of processes in the field of Electronics and Communications Engineering.

Confront what is promising

Discover fresh technologies and innovations in a variety of fields, as of nanotechnology to biotechnology. Electronical engineers come across innovative ways to utilize electronic materials and magnetic phenomena like signals and radio waves to build people’s lives improved.

Electronics and Communications engineers produce practical pioneering solutions and work with other smart, rousing people to create, propose and put up new technologies and products that matter. ECEs design sophisticated technologies and goods that play a huge role in this modern era such as smart phones, tablet computers, wireless networks, medical devices, and video games, high-fidelity audio and 3D television.
