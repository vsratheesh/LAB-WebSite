Overview

PSU Jobs

The only jobs that provide a constant source of income are government jobs. PSU’s allow you to get into core sector. If you are into PSUs you get a chance to improve your skills that were learnt in engineering. The possible reasons why people are interested in getting into PSUs are:

  • Attractive basic salary which is around 8Lakhs/annum
  • Minimal work pressure
  • Job security

Though many B.Tech graduates want to get into PSU’s, they really do not have any idea about this sector. Further in this article you will read types of PSU’s, the recruitment procedures, eligibility, career growth etc.

PSUs are divided into 3 categories based on their yearly turn-over of the organization. They are

  • Maharatna (7)
  • Miniratna (17)
  • Navaratana (56)
Ten Things

Eligibility criteria

Academic Qualification:B.Tech in Electronics and communication Engineering

Age Limit:Varies from company to company but generally lies between 21 and 30 years.

Work Experience:Not required for many posts but adds weight during interview and also helps during posting.

Physical Standards:General physical standards are not mentioned. These may vary from role to role. Physical standards may high for floor jobs.

How to Enter

Entrance Exam:General English, General Awareness, Current Affairs and Technical knowledge in the Electronics& communication engineering subjects.

Apply Directly:Students can apply directly to companies which specify the mode of application and entrance exam in the notification.

Through A Reference: Depends on company. Some companies accept applications through reference.

How to Prepare

Written Exam:

  • General Studies/GK – Manorama Year Book,
  • General English – R S Sharma.
  • Electronics & communication Engineering –Engineering books.

    • Personal Interview: Only some PSU/PSEs have personal interviews. These interviews are generally purely technical in nature. You should also read about company profile.

Skill Set

These jobs are primarily technical in nature. However, those who are selected as management trainees will become managers in future. Those selected as MT will be trained by the company as they progress in their career.

Job Description and Designation

Graduate Engineer, Management Trainee, Engineer Trainee and Executive Trainee are the entry-level designation. Fresh recruits are expected to perform technical tasks such as production, design, etc. of machines.

Salary and Perks

Salary and perks vary from company to company. Minimum salary is Rs. 25,000 and may go up to Rs. 70,000/- in Maharatna and Navaratna companies. Some top PSUs such as Indian Oil Corporation (IOC) have unparalleled perks.

Pros and Cons

Pros Cons

The Don’ts

  • Don’t lose patience. In PSUs, career growth may not be as fast as in private companies.
  • Don’t despair with posting in remote areas. Though in initial years, you’ll be posted in remote areas, as you grow in career, you’ll be posted in metro areas.

Career Switch

  • You can enter IES, private sector companies or with the experience of working in public sector, you can choose other careers such as Civil Services, etc.
Know More

Quality of Work

  • You would get to learn and work with some really good technical stuff for in training or one year or so.
  • Tip: To learn the work, sometimes you need to work from the scratch to know about all the things in the organization (Be open to learn in any organization to achieve long term goals).

Compensation and Benefits:

  • As PSUs process the best work environment as well as pay high in terms of salaries.This is the best place to start a good career; you get ample cash plus benefits like quarters with free electricity, allowance, low interest on loans for cars, house, furnishing, mobiles etc.

Job satisfaction:

  • Your bonus and promotions are fixed on the basis of your qualification and work structure. If you want to grow in this sector, you should upgrade yourself with degrees
Entry Level Positions

Public sector undertakings or public sector enterprise are State-Owned enterprises in India. PSUs recruit Mechanical Engineers for various posts at entry level like

  • Graduate Engineer
  • Management Trainee
  • Executive Trainee
  • Engineer Trainee, etc

Recruitments for these posts will be on GATE score for most of the companies and other companies conducts their own entrance exam based on their requirements for selecting candidates

Selection Process

Bharat Electronics Limited

BEL hires Electronics & communication engineering graduates for the post of Probationary engineer. Notification will be announced generally in August – September. Minimum of 60% aggregate in B.Tech is eligible to apply. Candidates called for the group discussion and interview based on GATE score. Minimum pay scale at the initial level is 4.8/- lakhs per annum.

National Aluminum Company Limited

NALCO recruits ECE graduates for the post of Graduate Engineer. Notification for this post will be released in August or September. Candidates will be called for the group discussion and interview considering GATE score. You have to get minimum of 65% aggregate in B.Tech. In NALCO a graduate engineer can get pay scale of 27, 027/- per month.

Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited

HPCL recruits ECE graduates for the post of Gradate engineer based on GATE score. Every year notification for this post releases in August or September. Candidates will be called for the Group discussion and interview considering the GATE score. Eligibility to get in to HPCL is you have to gat minimum aggregate of 60% for general and OBC it varies for others. CTC will be around 8.5 lakhs per annum.

Bharat Heavy ECE Limited (BHEL)

BHEL hires ECE graduates for the post of Engineer trainees. Notification for this post will be released in August or September. Considering the GATE score you will be called for the group discussion and Interview. Minimum eligibility for a B.Tech graduate is 60% aggregate. You will be getting an initial pay of 24,900/- per month.

Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited

BPCL hires ECE graduates for entry level positions based on GATE score. Candidate will be called for the interview considering the GATE rank. You have to go for Psychometric Test to assess candidates on behavioural dimensions. Eligibility is 60% aggregate in engineering. At the initial level the basic pay per month is Rs.24, 900 – 50,500.

Oil and Natural Gas Corporation

ONGC recruits ECE graduates to fill Graduate Trainee position. Notification will be released in November or December every year. Candidates are called for the interview based on GATE score. Minimum 60% aggregate candidates are eligible. The pay at the initial level is of Rs. 24,900 – 50,500/- per month.

National Thermal Power Corporation Ltd

NTPC hires ECE graduates to fill the entry level posts. Based on GATE score candidates are called for the interview. Minimum eligibility is 65% to get in to NTPC. Initial pay will be around 24,000/- per month.

Coal India Ltd

Coal India Ltd. hires ECE graduates for Management Trainee position. They will conduct own Entrance exam to hire candidates.

The written test shall be of three (03) hours duration comprising of 2 Papers

  • Paper I:General Awareness & Aptitude

  • Paper II: Technical aptitude in the relevant discipline

Each paper shall consist of 100 questions with 100 marks. Minimum marks to clear the exam are 40 for OC people it varies for others. The candidates selected in written exam are eligible to attend the interview.

Minimum eligibility is 60% in B.Tech. Initial pay will be around Rs. 20,600 – 46,500/- per month.

Power Grid Corporation of India Limited

PGCIL hires ECE graduates to fill the entry level position based on GATE score. Notifications releases in August or September. Candidates will be called for the interview based on GATE score. Candidates have to get minimum of 65% aggregate in their B.Tech. Initial pay will be around Rs. 24900-50500/- per month.

Neyveli Lignite Corporation limited

NLC hires ECE graduates to fill entry level positions. Candidates will be called for the interview based on GATE score. Candidates with minimum 60% aggregate are eligible. CTC will be around 8.12 lakhs per annum.

Pros&Cons

Pros

  • There is no extra working hours and stable source of income.
  • Promotions based on your work.
  • Pension facility is available when you retire.
  • Free medical facility for you and your family members with minimum accommodation charges.
  • Being a Government employee you will have reorganization/status in society.

Cons

  • Salary growth will be slow when compared with IT sector.
  • If you have any ambition in your life to do something new you cannot achieve that as you are in to Government job.
  • You have to do respective work every day, jobs will not be challenging to you.
  • You might have to deal with corruption in some form or the other.
