Overview

PSU Overview

The only jobs that provide a constant source of income are government jobs. PSU’s allow you to get into core sector. If you are into PSUs you get a chance to improve your skills that were learnt in engineering. The possible reasons why people are interested in getting into PSUs are:

  • Attractive basic salary which is around 8Lakhs/annum
  • Minimal work pressure
  • Job security

Though many B.Tech graduates want to get into PSU’s, they really don’t have any idea about this sector.

PSUs are divided into 3 categories based on their yearly turn-over of the organization. They are

  • Maharatna (7)
  • Miniratna (17)
  • Navaratana (56)
Ten Things

Eligibility criteria

Academic Qualification:B.Tech in EIE Engineering

Age Limit:Varies from company to company but generally lies between 21 and 30 years.

Work Experience:Not required for many posts but adds weight during interview and also helps during posting.

Physical Standards:General physical standards are not mentioned. These may vary from role to role. Physical standards may high for floor jobs.

How to enter?

Entrance Exam: General English, General Awareness, Current Affairs and Technical knowledge in the EIE engineering subjects.

Apply Directly: Students can apply directly to companies which specify the mode of application and entrance exam in the notification.

Through A Reference:Depends on company. Some companies accept applications through reference.

How to enter?

Written Exam:

  • General Studies/GK – Manorama Year Book
  • General English – R S Sharma.
  • EIE Engineering –Engineering books.

Personal Interview:Only some PSU/PSEs have personal interviews. These interviews are generally purely technical in nature. You should also read about company profile.

Skill Set

These jobs are primarily technical in nature. However, those who are selected as management trainees will become managers in future. Those selected as MT will be trained by the company as they progress in their career.

Job Description & Designation

Graduate Engineer, Management Trainee, Engineer Trainee and Executive Trainee are the Rajesh-level designation. Fresh recruits are expected to perform technical tasks such as production, design, etc. of machines.

Salary and Perks

Salary and perks vary from company to company. Minimum salary is Rs. 25,000 and may go up to Rs. 70,000/- in Maharatna and Navaratna companies. Some top PSUs such as Indian Oil Corporation (IOC) have unparalleled perks.

Pros&Cons

Pros Cons
Stable source of income Slow career growth
Performance-based incentives Limited scope
Pension Not challenging
Medical & education facilities for family Posting in remote areas

The Don'ts

  • Don’t lose patience. In PSUs, career growth may not be as fast as in private companies.
  • Don’t despair with posting in remote areas. Though in initial years, you’ll be posted in remote areas, as you grow in career, you’ll be posted in metro areas.

Career Switch

  • You can enter IES, private sector companies or with the experience of working in public sector, you can choose other careers such as Civil Services, etc.

Entry Level Positions

Public sector undertakings or public sector enterprise are State-Owned enterprises in India. PSUs recruit EIE Engineers for various posts at entry level like

  • Graduate Engineer
  • Management Trainee
  • Executive Trainee
  • Engineer Trainee, etc.

Recruitments for these posts will be on GATE score for most of the companies and other companies conducts their own entrance exam based on their requirements for selecting candidates.

List of Companies

Bharat Electronics Limited

BEL recruits EIE engineers for the post of Probationary engineer based on the GATE score. The students will be called for the Group discussion and interview in the ratio of 1:3. Student with 60% marks (General/OBC) 55% for SC/ST/PWD are eligible to apply. Grade pay will be 16,400 to 40,500. After adding all benefits CTC will be around 4.8 lakh per annum.

National Aluminum Company Limited (NALCO)

Nalco is a Navaratna PSU company. Nalco hires EIE engineers for the post of Graduate engineer based on GATE score. Students will be called for the interview in the ratio of 1:3. Notification for this post will be in the month of august or September. A student with 65 %( for general) 55% (for others) aggregate is eligible for this post. Salary at the Rajesh level is 16,400 to 40,500/- per month.

Gas Authority of India Ltd

Gas authority of India Ltd. hires EIE students for the post of Executive trainee position. Selection for this post is based on GATE score. Candidates will be called for the Group discussion and Interview based on GATE score. The students with 65% aggregate are eligible for this post. Pay scale will be around 9 to 11.8 lakhs per annum.

Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited

HPCL hires EIE engineers for the Graduate engineer position. Students will be called based on GATE score and then company conducts group discussion and Interview. Eligibility for the candidate is minimum 60% aggregate for general/OBC and 50% for others.CTC will be around 8.5 lakhs per annum

Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited

BHEL hires EIE engineers for the post of Engineer Trainees in the month of January or February. Notification for these posts releases by BHEL in the month of august or September. Candidates will be called based on GATE score. Eligible candidates will be called for group discussion and personnel interview in the ratio of 1:3.

Minimum 60% marks in B.Tech or Five years integrated Master’s degree or Dual degree programme in Engineering or Technology. Maximum age limit for graduates is 27 years for graduates and 29 years for post graduates. CTC will be between 8 to 9 lakhs per annum. In the training period salary will be around 2, 50,000/-.

Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited

BPCL hires EIE students for the post of Management Trainee. Notifications will be released in the month of August or September. Based on GATE score candidates will be called for

  • Group discussion
  • Psychometric tests to assess candidates on behavioral dimensions
  • Personnel Interview

Candidates with minimum of 60% aggregate for General/OBC and 50% for SC/ST/PWD. Basic pay is 24,900 to 50,500 per month.

Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Ltd

ONGC hires EIE students for the post of Graduate Trainee generally notification for this post will be released in November or December. Candidates will be selected on the basis of their performance in GATE followed by interview. Candidates with minimum of 60% aggregate are eligible. Salary will be 24,900 to 50,500/- per month with an increment of 3% per year.

National Thermal Power Corporation Limited

NTPC hires EIE graduates for the Rajesh level posts. Candidates will be called for the group discussion and interview based on GATE score. The students with minimum of 65%marks are eligible to apply. Pay scale will be around 24,900 to 50,500 per month.

Indian Oil Corporation Ltd

IOCL hires EIE students for the post of Graduate engineers on the basis of GATE score further selection process will be group discussion and personnel interview. Notification will be released in August or September. Candidates with 65% aggregate are eligible. CTC is around 9 lakhs per annum.

Coal India Limited (CIL)

Coal India Ltd. hires EIE graduates for the post of Management Trainee. The students with 60% aggregate can apply for this post. Candidate has to clear the written exam and then he will be called for the Personnel interview. Pay scale will be around 20,600 to 46,500 per month

Steel Authority of India Limited

SAIL hires EIE graduates for post of Management Trainee. Candidates with 55% aggregate and age below 30 years are eligible to apply. Selection will be based on written test and personnel interview. The candidates who scored 50 percentile marks in written exam are eligible for interview and GD. Pay scale for this post is around 20,600 to 46,500/- per month.

Bharat Earth Movers Limited

BEML hires EIE students at the fresher level. Candidates will be selected based on the written exam and Personnel interview. Candidates with 60% aggregate are eligible to apply. Pay scale for this post is around 12,600 to 32,500 per month.

How to enter

Most of the PSU companies recruiting students on the basis of GATE score. So you need to enter well for GATE exam as the competition is very tough you need to put the best efforts to get the job in PSUs. Apart from core subjects you need to learn General awareness, English and Aptitude, in mechanical engineering most important topics are strength of material, manufacturing, applied thermodynamics.

If your aim is to get in to PSUs make sure that you get a rank below 200 in GATE. To get good rank in you have to answer maximum questions and score more. While preparing for GATE doesn’t skip any subjects. Try to solve previous year GATE papers it will help you a lot, you will get clear idea about important topics and difficulty level by solving previous papers. Always revise the studied topics.

Pros&Cons

Pros

  • There is no extra working hours and stable source of income.
  • Promotions based on your work.
  • Pension facility is available when you retire
  • Free medical facility for you and your family members with minimum accommodation charges.
  • Being a Govt employee you will have reorganization/status in society.

Cons

  • Salary growth will be slow when compared with IT sector.
  • If you have any ambition in your life to do something new you cannot achieve that as you are in to Govt job
  • You have to do respective work every day, jobs will not be challenging to you.
  • You might have to deal with corruption in some form or the other.
