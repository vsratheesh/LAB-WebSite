Overview

PSU Jobs

The only jobs that provide a constant source of income are government jobs. PSU’s allow you to get into core sector. If you are into PSUs you get a chance to improve your skills that were learnt in engineering. The possible reasons why people are interested in getting into PSUs are

  • Attractive basic salary which is around 8Lakhs/annum
  • Minimal work pressure
  • Job security

Though many B.Tech graduates want to get into PSU’s, they really do not have any idea about this sector. Further in this article you will read types of PSU’s, the recruitment procedures, eligibility, career growth etc,.

PSUs are divided into 3 categories based on their yearly turn-over of the organization. They are

  • Maharatna (7)
  • Miniratna (17)
  • Navaratana (56)
Ten Things

Eligibility criteria

Academic Qualification: B.Tech in EIE

Varies from company to company but generally lies between 21 and 30 years.

Work Experience: Not required for many posts but adds weight during interview and also helps during posting.

Physical Standard: Not general physical standards are mentioned. May vary from role to role. Standards maybe high for floor jobs.

How to Enter

Entrance Exam: General English, General Awareness, Current Affairs and Instrumentation Engineering.

Apply Directly: Students can apply directly to companies which specify the mode of application and entrance exam in the notification.

Through A Reference: Depends on company. Some companies accept applications through reference.

How to Prepare

Written Exam: General Studies/GK – Manorama Year Book, General English – R S Sharma. EIE – Engineering books.

Personal Interview: Only some PSU/PSEs have personal interviews. These interviews are generally purely technical in nature. You should also read about company profile.

Skill Set

These jobs are primarily technical in nature. However, those who are selected as management trainees will become managers in future. Those selected as MT will be trained by the company as they progress in their career.

Job Description and Designation

Graduate Engineer, Management Trainee, Engineer Trainee and Executive Trainee are the entry-level designation. Fresh recruits are expected to perform technical tasks such as production, design, etc. of machines.

Salary and Perks

Salary and perks vary from company to company. Minimum salary is Rs. 25,000 and may go up to Rs. 70,000/- in Maharatna and Navratna companies. Some top PSUs such as Indian Oil Corporation (IOC) have unparalleled perks.

Career Progression

Engineer, Senior Engineer, Assistant Managers, Managers etc

Pros and Cons

Pros Cons
Stable source of income Slow career growth
Performance-based incentives Limited scope
Pension Not challenging
Medical & education facilities for family Posting in remote areas
Value in society Corruption

The Don’ts

Don’t lose patience. In PSUs, career growth may not be as fast as in private companies.

Don’t despair with posting in remote areas. Though in initial years, you’ll be posted in remote areas, as you grow in career, you’ll be posted in metro areas.

Career Switch

You can enter IES, private sector companies or with the experience of working in public sector, you can choose other careers such as Instrumentation Services, etc.

Know More

Quality of Work

  • You would get to learn and work with some really good technical stuff for in training or one year or so.
  • Tip: To learn the work, sometimes you need to work from the scratch to know about all the things in the organization (Be open to learn in any organization to achieve long term goals).

Compensation and Benefits:

  • As PSUs process the best work environment as well as pay high in terms of salaries.This is the best place to start a good career; you get ample cash plus benefits like quarters with free electricity, allowance, low interest on loans for cars, house, furnishing, mobiles etc.

Job satisfaction:

  • Your bonus and promotions are fixed on the basis of your qualification and work structure. If you want to grow in this sector, you should upgrade yourself with degrees
Entry Level Positions

Public sector undertakings or public sector enterprise are State-Owned enterprises in India. PSUs recruit Mechanical Engineers for various posts at entry level like

  • Graduate Engineer
  • Management Trainee
  • Executive Trainee
  • Engineer Trainee, etc

Recruitments for these posts will be on GATE score for most of the companies and other companies conducts their own entrance exam based on their requirements for selecting candidates

Interview Process

List of PSU companies that provide jobs for EIE given below

National Thermal Power Corporation Limited (NTPC)

NTPC recruits EIE for the post of Executive engineer based on the GATE score through NTPC Engineers Executive Trainee (EET) Exam. The students will be called for the Group discussion and interview in the ratio of 1:3. Student with 65% marks (General/O BC) 55% for SC/ST/PWD is eligible to apply. Notification for this post will be in the month of September or October Selected candidates will be placed in the pay scale of Rs. 24900-50500 at the basic pay of Rs. 24900/- during one-year training. These candidates will be absorbed after successful completion of training as Assistant Manager in the pay scale of Rs. 24900-3%-50500 at a basic pay of Rs. 25650/-

Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Ltd. (ONGC)

ONGC is a Maharatna PSU company. ONGC hires Instrumentation engineers for the post of Graduate Trainee (GT) based on GATE score. Students will be called for the interview in the ratio of 1:3. Notification for this post will be in the month of November and December. A student with 60 % aggregate is eligible for this post. The pay scale is in the grade of Rs. 24,900 – 50,500/- with an increment of 3% per year.

Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited

HPCL hires Instrumentation engineers for the Graduate Engineer (GE) position. Students will be called based on GATE score and then company conducts group discussion and Interview. Eligibility for the candidate is minimum 60% aggregate for general/OBC and 50% for others.CTC will be around 8.5 lakhs per annum.

National Aluminum Company Limited (NALCO)

NALCO is a Navaratna company which hires Instrumentation engineers for the Graduate Engineers (GE) position. Students will be called based on GATE score and then company conducts group discussion and Interview. Notification for this post will be in the month of August and September. Eligibility for the candidate is minimum 65% aggregate for general/OBC and 55% for others.CTC at minimum Pay scale is Rs. 27027/- per month.

Indian Oil Corporation Ltd.

IOCL hires Instrumentation students for the post of Graduate engineers on the basis of GATE score further selection process will be group discussion and personnel interview. Notification will be released in August or September. Candidates with 65% aggregate are eligible. CTC is around 9 lakhs per annum.

Steel Authority of India Limited

SAIL hires Instrumentation graduates for post of Management Trainee. Candidates with 55% aggregate and age below 30 years are eligible to apply. Selection will be based on written test and personnel interview. The candidates who scored 50 percentile marks in written exam are eligible for interview and GD. Pay scale for this post is around 20,600 to 46,500/- per month.

Delhi Metro Rail Corporation Ltd.

DMRC hires Instrumentation graduates for post of Jr. Engineer. Candidates with 60% aggregate and age below 30 years are eligible to apply. Selection will be based on GATE, GD and personnel interview. Pay scale for this post is around 13,500 to 25,520/- per month.

Vizag Steel Plant

Vizag Steel plant hires Instrumentation graduates for post of Management Trainee (Technical). Candidates with 60% aggregate and age below 40 years are eligible to apply. Selection will be based on written test, GD and personnel interview. Pay scale for this post is around 206000 to 46500/- per month.

Heavy Engineering Corporation Limited (HECL)

HECL hires Instrumentation graduates for post of Technical Graduate Engineers. Candidates with 60% aggregate are eligible to apply. Selection will be based on GATE and personnel interview. Pay scale for this post is around 16000 to 40000/- per month.

Electronics Corporation of India Limited (ECIL)

ECIL hires Instrumentation graduates for post of Graduate Engineers. Candidates with 65% aggregate are eligible to apply. Selection will be based on Written and personnel interview. Pay scale for this post is around 4 lakhs per annum.

Pros&Cons

Pros

  • There is no extra working hours and stable source of income.
  • Promotions based on your work.
  • Pension facility is available when you retire.
  • Free medical facility for you and your family members with minimum accommodation charges.
  • Being a Govt employee you will have reorganization/status in society.

Cons

  • Salary growth will be slow when compared with IT sector.
  • If you have any ambition in your life to do something new you cannot achieve that as you are in to Govt job.
  • You have to do respective work every day, jobs will not be challenging to you.
  • You might have to deal with corruption in some form or the other.
