Overview

Overview Government Jobs

People are very much interested in government jobs, especially middle class families. Government jobs are preferred because of its Job Security and Pay structure. Electronics and Instrumentation engineering students can get a job in any central and state government undertakings.

We will help you to choose a career with their respective eligibility in 10 easy steps. It helps you more in comparing yourself with the eligibility of any career. This document will easily help you to hold a knowledge on how to enter into a government sector, its eligibility criteria, career growth, pay pack etc,.

Ten Things

Eligibility criteria

Academic Qualification: B.Tech in Instrumentation Engineering.

Age Limit: 21 – 30 years for general category. It is higher for other categories such as SC, ST, OBC and Physically Handicapped.

Work Experience:Both experienced and freshers are taken in government jobs. Job notifications clearly mention the number of years of experience required to apply for the job.

Physical Standards:Physical and medical standards are mentioned in the notification as per the recruitment.

How to Enter

Entrance Exam: General English, General Awareness, Current Affairs and Instrumentation Engineering

Apply Directly:In most cases, you can apply to the department or organization which advertises vacancies. In some others, selection is done through a separate body.

Through A Reference:Depends on company. Some companies accept applications through reference.

How to Prepare

Written Exam: General Studies/GK – Manorama Year Book, General English – R S Sharma. Instrumentation Engineering – B.Tech books. .

Personal Interview: Most jobs have a personal interview. Armed Forces select through a 5-day Services Selection Board interview.

Skill Set

Careers in government sector, especially Armed Forces require one to possess leadership qualities in addition to technical knowledge.

Job Description and Designation

Entry level designations include Assistant Engineer, Executive Engineer, etc. Fresh recruits are expected to perform technical tasks such as production, design, etc. of machines.

Salary and Perks

Salary at entry level is close to Rs.50,000. Engineers in the Indian Railways have facilities that even IAS and IPS officers envy. This includes housing, medical, education facilities for children, etc.

Career Progression

Progression in career depends on department, entry-level designation, number of years of service, etc.

Pros&Cons

Pros Cons
Attractive Perks Posting in remote areas
Pension Not challenging
Medical & education facilities for family Corruption
Better fixed timings (8 hours Max) Low growth
Respect in society Red tape

The Don’ts

  • Don’t lose patience. In government jobs, career growth may not be as fast as in private companies.
  • Don’t despair with posting in remote areas. Though in initial years, you’ll be posted in remote areas, as you grow in career, you’ll be posted in metro areas.

Career Switch

You can enter IES, private sector companies or with the experience of working in public sector, you can choose other careers such as Instrumentation Services, etc.

Why Govt Jobs

People are very much interested in government jobs, especially middle class families prefer getting in to government sector. Government jobs are preferred because the biggest advantage of government jobs is “Job Security” and Pay structure. Many people who worked in private sector after working 2 to 3 years want to get in Government jobs because of work stress.

SSC

SSC conducts Entry level examination to recruit engineers from different streams for recruiting Junior Engineers. Junior engineer Examination will be conducted every year in August or September.

Eligibility:Candidate should be completed his degree in Instrumentation engineering from a recognized university.

Age limit: Minimum age should be 18 years and maximum age for applying is 32 years.

SSC JE Pay Scale: Pay scale will be Rs. 9,300/- to 34,800/- + Grade Pay (GP) Rs. 4,200/-

How to apply?Eligible candidates can apply through official website. Both online and off line mode of applications are available.

How to prepare?

The Examination will be conducted in two stages

  • Written Test (500 marks)
  • Interview (100 marks)

Written Test:

Papers Subject Maximum Marks Duration
Paper-I Objective type (OMR Answer Sheets will be provided for this Paper) General Intelligence & Reasoning 50 2 Hours
General Awareness 50
General Engineering (Instrumentation) 100
Paper II  Conventional Type General Engineering (Instrumentation) 300 2 Hours

Syllabus:Questions will be asked from engineering subjects according to your stream.

Genco&Transco

GENCO conducts entry-level exam for B.Tech graduates. GENCO recruits freshers for the post of Assistant engineer.

Written Exam

  • Section A:Questions will be asked on core academic curriculum of respective discipline.

  • Section B:Analytical aptitude questions.

Assistant Engineer Qualifying Marks:Minimum qualifying marks in this exam for OC category-40%, BC-35%, SC/ST/PH category-30%.

Age:Minimum age to apply 18 years & Maximum age is 44 years.

Selection Process: Performance in the written examination will be measured along with the personal interview.

Pay Scale:41,155 – 63,600/-per month.

Research Sector

If you want to get in to research, DRDO, ISRO, BARC, etc are what you should aim for. These institutes conduct their own examinations maybe once in 2 or 3 years, depending on their requirements. Exam and interview will be tougher when compared with other Exams. In the interview, a panel of scientists sit in front of you and ask you the questions, usually as a rapid fire. But, if you are well prepared with the basics, you shouldn't have any trouble.

Scientist engineer

The following companies will be recruiting Instrumentation engineers for the post of scientist engineer based on the NET/GATE score.

  • DRDO
  • CSIR
  • ICMR
  • CCMB
  • ICHR
  • NCCS
  • IGCAR
  • UGC

Job Description (JRF):Research fellows are graduate degree holders, who perform academic research. By devoting all of their time to research, these professionals can act as investigators who initiate developments in their field of study. Research fellows must meet several education and skills requirements to do their jobs effectively for universities and other research-oriented organizations.

Eligibility:Eligible candidates should possess First class graduate degree in professional course (B.E./ B.Tech) or with NET/ GATE

Age Limit:Candidate’s upper age limit should be maximum 28 years as on last date for receipt of application.

Selection Process: Candidates will be selected based on their performance in the interview.

Pay scale: 16000-30000/-per month.

Career Progression: Senior Research Fellowship (SRF)

RRB

Every year Railway sector conducts their own entrance exam to fill the vacancies in different modules. Railway recruitment board recruits Instrumentation engineers for the post of senior section engineer.

Senior section Engineer (Instrumentation)

Age limit:The minimum age to apply for RRB exam is 20 years and maximum age is 34 Years.

Pay scale: RS 9300-34800 + GP 4600/- per month.

Qualification:Candidate should have completed his Instrumentation engineering from a recognised university.

Selection Process: Written Examination, Document Verification and Medical test.

  • Selection is made strictly as per merit, on the basis of written examination.
  • The Question papers shall be of Objective Multiple Choice Type. The questions paper will be in English, Hindi, Urdu and local languages.
  • Questions for the written exam will be generally from the educational standards. Objective type multiple choice questions base on General Awareness, Arithmetic, General Intelligence and reasoning, General Science and Technical Ability.
  • For each wrong answer 1/3 marks allotted for each question shall be deducted. The question paper will consist of 150 questions.

Career path

  • Assistant Instrumentation Engineer(Gaz.)
  • Divisional Instrumentation Engineer
  • Senior Divisional Instrumentation Engineer

Job description:

  • Senior section engineer is known as supervisor in Instrumentation department of Railways. Generally senior section engineer can be posted either station or production units/workshops.

  • He is known as Chief Train Examinerin Stations.

  • He will be taking the responsibility of maintenance of trains. It is his duty to see that Goods wagons and passenger coaches are in good fettle and ready in time for train service as required by the operating personnel.
  • He will be having railway technicians working under him to do the job. He will be supervising the work done by the technicians.
  • In workshops he will be the in charge for periodic overhauling/ production components of loco/wagon/coaches.

How to Apply: Eligible Interested Candidates are required to apply Online through any one of Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) Website.

Physical standards:

  • Physically fit in all aspects.
  • Visual Standards —Distance Vision: 6/6, 6/6 without glasses.
  • Near Vision: Sn: 0.6,0.6 without glasses (must clear fogging test) and must pass test for Colour Vision, Binocular Vision, Field of Vision & Night Vision.

Different posts for Instrumentation graduates in RRB are:

  • Assistant Engineer
  • Jr. Engineer
  • Executive Engineer
  • Product Test Engineer
