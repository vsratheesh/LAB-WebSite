What is Mech?

Mechanical Engineering is the largest of all engineering branches,including zones such as energy, fluid mechanics, dynamics, combustion, vibration, design, manufacturing process, systems modeling and replication, mechatronics, robotics, mechanics of material, rapid protottyping and combinations.
Mechanical engineering

is a miscellaneous subject that originates its scope from the necessity to design and producethe whole thing from small discrete parts and devices to huge systems. The part of a mechanical engineer is to take a creation from an idea to the open market. In order to achieve this, a wide-range of skills is obligatory.The mechanical engineer wants to attain specific skills and knowledge. He needs to realize the forces and the current situation that a creation, its parts,or its subsystems will meet; to project them for functionality,aesthetics,and the aptitude to endure the forces and the current situation they will be exposed to; and to control the best way to create them and confirm that they will function without a botch.Possibly that one skill for a mechanical engineer’s limited domain is the skill to examine and plan objects and systems with motion.

Mechanical engineering is the combination of creativity, knowledge and analytical tools to complete the difficult task of shaping an idea into reality. Mechanical engineering involves the application of generation, distribution, and use of energy; the processing of materials; the control and automation of manufacturing systems; the design and development of machines; and the solutions to environmental problems.

It work on a wide range of products and systems, for example, space shuttle vehicles, aircraft of all sizes and shapes, automobiles, turbines, pumps, power plants, and factories.
