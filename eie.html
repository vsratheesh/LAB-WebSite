What is EiE?

Instrumentation Engineering is a specialized branch of Electrical and Instrumentation Engineering. Instrumentation technology helps create, construct and maintain measuring and control devices and systems found in
manufacturing plants and research institutions.

Any manufacturing demands Automation which requires sensing, monitoring and controlling of variables. Just imagine a world without instruments. It would be like traversing into a time that probably goes even beyond the Stone Age. Man has always been fascinated towards instruments and the quest to invent and innovate on new instruments has pushed mankind beyond barriers to an age where instruments have become a part and parcel of the life. Today, we find instruments at home and at workplace, and this was possible only because of the science instrumentation.
