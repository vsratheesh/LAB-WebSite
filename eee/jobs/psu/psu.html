Overview

PSU Jobs

The only jobs that provide a constant source of income are government jobs. PSU’s allow you to get into core sector. If you are into PSUs you get a chance to improve your skills that were learnt in engineering. The possible reasons why people are interested in getting into PSUs are

  • Attractive basic salary which is around 8Lakhs/annum
  • Minimal work pressure
  • Job security

Though many B.Tech graduates want to get into PSU’s, they really do not have any idea about this sector. Further in this article you will read types of PSU’s, the recruitment procedures, eligibility, career growth etc,.

PSUs are divided into 3 categories based on their yearly turn-over of the organization. They are

  • Maharatna (7)
  • Miniratna (17)
  • Navaratana (56)
l help you to choose a career with their respective eligibility in 10 easy steps. It helps you more in comparing yourself with the eligibility of any career. This document will easily help you to hold knowledge on how to get into IES and its eligibility criteria.

Ten Things

Eligibility criteria

Academic Qualification:B.Tech in Electrical Engineering

Age Limit:Varies from company to company but generally lies between 21 and 30 years.

Work Experience:Not required for many posts but adds weight during interview and also helps during posting.

Physical Standards:General physical standards are not mentioned. These may vary from role to role. Physical standards may high for floor jobs.

How to enter?

Entrance Exam: General English, General Awareness, Current Affairs and Technical knowledge in the Electrical engineering subjects.

Apply Directly: Students can apply directly to companies which specify the mode of application and entrance exam in the notification.

Through A Reference:Depends on company. Some companies accept applications through reference.

How to prepare?

Written Exam:

  • General Studies/GK – Manorama Year Book
  • General English – R S Sharma.
  • Electrical Engineering –Engineering books.

Personal Interview:Only some PSU/PSEs have personal interviews. These interviews are generally purely technical in nature. You should also read about company profile.

Skill Set

These jobs are primarily technical in nature. However, those who are selected as management trainees will become managers in future. Those selected as MT will be trained by the company as they progress in their career.

Job Description & Designation

Graduate Engineer, Management Trainee, Engineer Trainee and Executive Trainee are the entry-level designation. Fresh recruits are expected to perform technical tasks such as production, design, etc. of machines.

Salary and Perks

Salary and perks vary from company to company. Minimum salary is Rs. 25,000 and may go up to Rs. 70,000/- in Maharatna and Navaratna companies. Some top PSUs such as Indian Oil Corporation (IOC) have unparalleled perks.

The Don'ts

  • Don’t lose patience. In PSUs, career growth may not be as fast as in private companies.
  • Don’t despair with posting in remote areas. Though in initial years, you’ll be posted in remote areas, as you grow in career, you’ll be posted in metro areas.

Career Switch

  • You can enter IES, private sector companies or with the experience of working in public sector, you can choose other careers such as Civil Services, etc.

Entry Level Positions

Public sector undertakings or public sector enterprise are State-Owned enterprises in India. PSUs recruit Mechanical Engineers for various posts at entry level like

  • Graduate Engineer
  • Management Trainee
  • Executive Trainee
  • Engineer Trainee, etc

Recruitments for these posts will be on GATE score for most of the companies and other companies conducts their own entrance exam based on their requirements for selecting candidates

Selection Process

Coal India Limited

Coal India Limited hires Electrical engineers for the post of Management Trainee in the month of May and June. Minimum Eligibility is 60% aggregate for a B.Tech graduate to apply. Candidates will be called for the interview based on written exam, the exam consist of two papers with 3 hours duration. Pay scale at the initial level will be 20,600 to 46,500/- per month

National Thermal Power Corporation

NTPC hires electrical graduates for the post of Engineers Executive Trainees based on the GATE. Candidates will be called for the group discussion and interview based on GATE score. Generally notifications for this post are announced in September or October. Minimum eligibility should be 65% aggregate in Engineering. Pay scale at the initial level will be Rs. 24900-3%-50500 at the basic pay of Rs. 24900/- during one-year training.

Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Ltd.

ONGC recruits Electrical engineers for the position of Graduate Trainee based on GATE exam. Candidates are called for the interview based on the GATE score. Notification for this post will be announced in November or December. Candidates with minimum of 60% aggregate in B.Tech are eligible. Pay scale at the initial stage is Rs. 24,900 – 50,500/-per month with an increment of 3% per year.

Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited

BHEL hires electrical engineers for the post of Engineer Trainees based on GATE score. Candidates are called for the group discussion and interview on the basis of GATE score. Notifications for this post will be announced in August or September. Candidates with minimum of 60% aggregate in B.Tech are eligible. CTC will be around 8 to 9 lakhs per annum, during the training period (1 year) it is 20,600/-per month.

Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited

HPCL hires electrical engineers for the post of Graduate Engineers based on gate score followed by group discussion and interview. Minimum of 60% aggregate in B.Tech are eligible. Notifications for this post will be announced in August or September. CTC will be Rs. 8.5 lakhs per annum. Stipend will be Rs. 33,000/- per month during the Training period.

National Aluminium Company Ltd

NACL hires electrical engineers for the post of Graduate Engineers based on GATE score followed by group discussion and interview. Notifications are announced in December or January. Minimum 65% marks in BE/ B.Tech for General/OBC Candidates and 55% for others. Pay scale will be Rs. 16,400 – 40,500 with other benefits

Power Grid Corporation of India Limited

PGCIL hires electrical engineers for the post of Executive Trainees based on the GATE score followed by group discussion and Interview. Notification for this post will be announced in August or September. Minimum eligibility is 65% for general/OBC and 50% for others. Pay scale will be Rs. 24900-50500/- per month.

Gas Authority of India Ltd

GAIL hires electrical engineers for the post of Executive Trainees based on the GATE score followed by group discussion and Interview. Notifications are announced in August or September. Minimum eligibility is 65% for General /OBC, 60% for others. Pay scale at the initial level will be 24, 900/- per month.

Indian Oil Corporation

IOCL hires electrical students for the post of Engineers/Graduate Apprentice Engineers based on GATE score followed by group discussion and interview. Notification will be announced in August and September. Minimum 65% aggregate in B.Tech for general/OBC it varies for others. Cost-to-Company (CTC), will be around Rs.9.00 lakhs per annum with Basic Pay of Rs.24900/- per month.

Pros&Cons

Pros

  • There is no extra working hours and stable source of income.
  • Promotions based on your work.
  • Pension facility is available when you retire.
  • Free medical facility for you and your family members with minimum accommodation charges.
  • Being a Government employee you will have reorganization/status in society.

Cons

  • Salary growth will be slow when compared with IT sector.
  • If you have any ambition in your life to do something new you cannot achieve that as you are in to Government job.
  • You have to do respective work every day, jobs will not be challenging to you.
  • You might have to deal with corruption in some form or the other.
